# Target Acquisition: Automating Research & The Perfect Briefing

## Session Overview

In this economy, "Capital" comes in many forms: a VC check, a client contract, or a strategic salary to extend your runway. Regardless of the source, the mechanism to get it is the same: Deep Research + A Tailored Pitch.

Most of us walk into high-stakes meetings with nothing but a quick LinkedIn scan. In this roundtable, we'll explore how to use AI to build a "dossier" on your target in 5 minutes instead of 5 hours, ensuring you hit the "hidden signals" that actually close the deal.

## What We'll Discuss

- The "Sniper" Scope: Finding specific information (recent podcasts, technical pain points) that isn't on a LinkedIn "About" page.
- The 5-Minute Dossier: Building an automated "Briefing Note" that appears in your inbox 1 hour before a meeting.
- The Narrative Pivot: Using AI to adapt your story—whether you are rewriting a bio for a "Whale" client or a resume for a "Bridge" role.

## Who Should Attend

We will split into groups based on your current approach:

- The Detective (Builder): For those doing manual research who need a faster system to find the facts.
- The Data Hoarder (Optimizer): For those with tools (Clay, Apollo) who have too much data and need to filter for the "signal."
- The Context Engineer (Strategist): For those who need help using AI to weave data into a winning pitch.

This is a focused, confidential roundtable, not a lecture. We are a proudly bilingual and multicultural community, flowing between English and Spanish.
