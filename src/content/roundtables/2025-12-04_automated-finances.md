# Automated Finances

## Session Overview

In this roundtable session, we'll explore how to automate your financial operations using AI and modern tools. Learn how founders are saving time and reducing errors by automating bookkeeping, invoicing, expense tracking, and financial reporting.

## What We'll Discuss

- AI-powered bookkeeping and accounting automation
- Automated invoicing and payment collection systems
- Expense tracking and categorization tools
- Financial reporting and dashboard automation
- Tax preparation automation strategies

## Who Should Attend

This session is perfect for:
- Founders struggling with financial admin work
- Small business owners looking to streamline operations
- Anyone wanting to reduce manual financial tasks

## Format

This is an interactive roundtable discussion where participants share experiences and learn from each other. Come prepared to share your own financial automation challenges and wins.
