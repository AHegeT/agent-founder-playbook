# The State of AI in 2026: The Friction, the Sweet Spot and the Future

## Session Overview

The flashy AI honeymoon phase is over. As builders, we've lived the friction firsthand. Agents and Founders is back from hiatus to explore the pragmatic middle ground: acknowledging the heavy friction, uncovering what actually works, and mapping out what's next now that the dust is settling.

Rather than a lecture, this is a roundtable to share our experiences, tools, and resources. Come ready to share your current, raw business challenges.

## What We'll Discuss

- **The Friction** - Where are agents failing you? Let's talk about abandoned initiatives, fragile multi-step logic, and tool instability.
- **The Sweet Spot** - What is actually driving ROI right now? (e.g., local RAG pipelines, deterministic code wrappers, or internal automations).
- **The Future** - What do we want to keep? Let's talk about the challenges ahead for our pipelines, what we can wait for frontier models to solve, and what we should be building ourselves.

## Who Should Attend

This session is perfect for:
- Founders and builders who have shipped AI features and hit real friction
- Anyone deciding where to invest (or stop investing) in agentic workflows
- Operators looking to separate hype from what's actually driving ROI today

## Format

This is an interactive roundtable discussion where participants share experiences and learn from each other. Come ready to share your current, raw business challenges.
