## Session Overview

In this roundtable session, we'll explore how to leverage AI tools and strategies to build more meaningful professional connections. We'll discuss practical approaches to networking that go beyond traditional methods.

### What We'll Discuss

- **Using AI to research and personalize outreach** - Learn how to use AI tools to gather insights about people before reaching out
- **Automating follow-ups while maintaining authenticity** - Discover systems that keep you connected without feeling robotic
- **Building systems to nurture long-term relationships** - Create workflows that help you stay in touch with your network
- **Tools and workflows that actually work** - Share and learn about practical tools being used by successful networkers

### Who Should Attend

This session is perfect for:
- Founders looking to build strategic partnerships
- Sales professionals wanting to improve their networking
- Anyone interested in using AI to enhance their professional relationships

### Format

This is an interactive roundtable discussion where participants share experiences and learn from each other. Come prepared to share your own networking challenges and successes.
